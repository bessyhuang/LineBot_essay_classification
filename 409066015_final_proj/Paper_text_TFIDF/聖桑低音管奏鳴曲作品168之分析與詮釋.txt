聖桑低音管奏鳴曲作品168之分析與詮釋
The Analysis and performance Interpretation of C.Saint-Saens for Bassoon and Piano Op.168
聖桑、低音管、奏鳴曲、作品168

聖桑雖然只寫作一首低音管奏鳴曲，但在低音管的曲目中，佔有一席之地；優美的旋律、豐富的和聲及寬廣音域的運用，是筆者選擇作為論文題目的原因，希望透過聖桑的生平、音越風格、創作動機及樂曲分析等方面來探討，以期能對此作品能作更好的演奏詮釋。
The beautiful melody,abundant harmony,and the use of broad music compass made me pick it as the topic of dissertation.I hope to make better interpretation of this compasition through the discussion of the composer's brief biographical sketch,the music style,his writing motive and the analysis of music.
