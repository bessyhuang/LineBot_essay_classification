Opensource架構下數位版權管理之研究
The Study of Digital Rights Management in Open Source Infrastructure
開放原始碼、數位版權管理
Open Source是近年新興的議題，除了社群之外，許多大型軟硬體商業公司紛紛響應開放原始碼活動。廠商可藉加值活動過程零售Linux發行套件，亦可提供售後服務支援，以年費訂閱與專業服務的方式售予企業客戶完整解決方案。其中包含以ASP模式的文件線上銷售服務。數位化後的內容檔案如果沒有經過妥善的保護，就可以輕易經由網路途徑散佈，造成未經合法授權使用的情形發生。而傳統對文件使用密碼鎖定開啟權限的方式已經不足以應付現代軟體的破解技。因此，結合執行權管制與加密技術的數位版權管理技術隨之而生。    本研究思考ASP型態的文件銷售服務可能遭遇到的問題，針對文件版權提出一個在Open Source架構下的解決方案，結合公開金鑰基礎建設和存取控制的技術，提供企業一個低成本且具有彈性的管理機制來保護重要文件。這個數位版權管理機制的特點有：利用身分認證和存取控制對於文件控管具有較高的彈性，簡化大量帳號權限設定的麻煩、支援國際標準OpenDocument檔案格式以及透過LDAP應用的公開金鑰基礎架構，提供一個安全的檔案傳輸環境，使系統運作更有效率。
Recently, open source is a hot topic, many large-scale software and hardware company response to open source except community. In the value-added activities, the company can sell Linux distribution and provide after-sell service using subscribe and professional service way to sell integration solution including document sale on line as application service provide. If there is not a well-arranged protection the digital content was easily diffused by Internet and unauthorization happened. Using password to lock the open right is not strong enough to avoid cracking skill. Therefore, the digital rights management combine access control with data encryption comes to meet the requirement.   This research paper find out the problem of selling document on line as application service provider and propose a solution of digital rights management in open source architecture combine public key infrastructure and access control to provide a low cost and flexible management mechanism to protect important document. The features of this digital rights management include (1) using authentication and access control make the management of documents flexible and simplify configuration of a large account; (2) support the international standard open document format; (3) the implementation of LDAP for public key infrastructure provides a secure transmission and makes the system more efficient.
