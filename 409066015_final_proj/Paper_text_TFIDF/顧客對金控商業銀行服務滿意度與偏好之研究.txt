顧客對金控商業銀行服務滿意度與偏好之研究
A Study of Consumers’ Satisfaction and Preference for Commercial Bank of Financial Holding Company
商業銀行、層級程序分析法、顧客滿意度、參照單位分析法、顧客偏好、聯合分析
在目前的金融市場中，金控商業銀行除了利用良好的成本控管與專業知識外，需再加上消費者認同的服務才有獲利空間。因此針對銀行所提供的服務進行顧客滿意度與偏好的研究。本研究藉由兩階段問卷發放進行調查。首先，第一階段決策因素問卷，尋找大台北地區具大學學歷以上且與銀行往來密切者為訪談對象，共計42位，並運用層級程序分析法找出受訪者對銀行服務滿意度各關鍵影響因素的權重，並據以設計第二階段滿意度問卷。第二階段滿意度問卷則以大台北地區不同背景且學歷為大學以上在職人員為研究對象，共計回收167份問卷，利用集群分析、參照單位分析及聯合分析探討不同族群對金控商業銀行的服務滿意度與偏好。研究結果如下：(一)  顧客依人口統計分為三特性不同的族群。(二)  滿意度各屬性當中，顧客最不滿意「客訴處理」的部分。(三)  顧客在選擇往來的金控商業銀行時主要考量因素為作業效率、服務品質與客訴處理。在顧客對銀行提供服務的偏好方面，最注重作業效率，其次為服務品質，最後為客訴處理。
In the financial market, financial holding companies make more profits relying on not only taking advantage of well-performing cost control and professional knowledge, but also knowing more about their customers. Therefore, this paper explores the relationship between the service banks provide and customers’ satisfaction.This paper divides the research into two parts. The first part uses AHP to analyze 42 customers with university or above degree and apply analytical hierarchy process for find out which key factors affect their satisfactions most. The second part,  based on the first part, targets at 167 customers with diversified background, and applies cluster analysis, relative to an identified distribution and conjoint analysis to explore their preferences.1.  Customers are divided into three groups by their demographic characteristics.2.  Among all attributes of the customer satisfaction, “customer complaint management” is less satisfied.3.  Customers select commercial bank of financial holding company based on the factor of efficiency, service quality and customer complaint management. Ordering of customers preference is efficiency, service quality and customer complaint management.
