以現金流量觀點探討企業獲利能力
An empirical study on corporate profitability by perspective of cash flow
現金流量、獲利能力、財務比率
電子業在政府政策發展下，已成為台灣產業中眾所囑目的明星，在上市櫃發行市場中，參與家數成長快速，其股票集中市場成交金額比例動輒超過80﹪。本研究希望藉由電子業中企業公開之財務資訊，並運用「現金流量」指標，以瞭解危機公司與成功公司間，在「現金流量」與「獲利能力」的關連性上是否有差異；並進而解析探討「現金流量」與「獲利能力」間之是否有高度關連性。本研究先針對電子業之「財務正常公司」及「財務危機公司」分類，並依其獲利能力探討其現金流量在「營運活動」、「投資活動」及「融資活動」間之變化，做出整理與趨勢變化；進而採用主成份分析（Principal Component Analysis）及逐步迴歸分析(Stepwise Regression)探討本研究所選取之15項自變數、5項干擾變數與6項獲利能力指標之關連性。經本研究實證後，危機公司與正常公司在「現金流量」與「獲利能力」的關連性上具有差異性；另經多變量分析後得知，共有11項自變數及干擾變數指標與獲利能力指標有關，且經分類後得知，此11項變數中有6項指標與營運活動有關。經本研究實證後得到之結論，企業應著重於本業的經營；且應有效的針對長期投資進行規劃，並注意資本結構的變化，避免過度負債經營，造成萬劫不復之窘境。
Owing to easy manipulation in accounting items, this study emphasis on the difference of cash flow and profitability betwwen failure companies and normal compnies.First, this study classified sampling companies into two types, normal companies and Failure Companies, and then analyzed the correlation, variration and trend between cash flow and profitability. This study used the Principle Component Analysis combined with Step-wise Regression Method to calculate the correlations among 15 independent variables, 5 moderate variables and 6 dependent variables chosen in this study.Results of this empirical study are shown as followingFirst, there is difference on correlation between cash flow and profitability compared by normal companies and failure companies.Secondly, after mutivariate analysis, there are 11 indepent variables and moderate variables having correlations with 6 dependet variables. And after classifying, there are 6 indepent variables and moderate variables having highly correlations with profitability.Finally, this study suggests corporates to focus on their core businiess and planning long-term investment effectively and watch out the capital structure change.
