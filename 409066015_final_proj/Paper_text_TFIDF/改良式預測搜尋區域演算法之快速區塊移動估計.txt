改良式預測搜尋區域演算法之快速區塊移動估計
Improved Predictive Search Area Approach for Fast Block motion estimation
區塊移動估計、區塊比對、搜尋區域、移動向量、估計準確性

現有的快速區塊移動估計演算法，大都集中在搜尋視窗內進行最佳的區塊比對計算。本論文採用預測搜尋區域演算法所預估的最小搜尋區域，藉由相鄰區塊影像的關聯性，以搜尋最小的區域來達到執行的效率。我們提出了三個改善方法：(1)在鄰近區塊移動向量的比對上，以目前區塊內的原始影像做比對，有效的減少預測搜尋區域演算法的預測誤差。(2)化簡最小搜尋區域比對法，我們採用先比對預測搜尋區域邊緣的部份，減少不必要的計算量。(3)針對影像左上方的初始區塊，我們採用較快速的六角形搜尋模型法來減少搜尋視窗內points比對的次數。本論文實驗的影像為Football與Tennis兩個影像片段。從實驗結果分析，本論文的方式除了有效的改善原本預測搜尋區域演算法產生的預測誤差與搜尋的計算量，並且相對於鑽石搜尋法、六角形搜尋模型法等快速區塊匹配移動估計演算法，維持較高的估計準確性，達到接近完全搜尋法的品質。
Most of the existing fast block motion estimation algorithm find the best block matching by search window. However, we adopt "A new predictive search area approach" algorithm to forecast the range of image motion. By means of the analysis of its correlation of neighboring block, the search for the lowest region can be processed more efficiently.    In this paper, we propose three improved methods for "A new predictive search area approach" algorithm. Firstly, we use the current block image to reduce predictive error ratio on neighboring block motion vector matching. Secondly, we simply search for the edge of the lowest region. In this case, it efficiently reduces unnecessary operation of candidate blocks. Thirdly, for the first block without the neighboring block motion vector, we use the "HEXBS search pattern" algorithm to reduce search points.Two real video footages are used in this paper, the football with 58 image frames and the tennis with 70 image frames. The results of our experiment proved that our proposed methods have efficiently solved "the predictive error ratio" of "A new predictive search area approach" algorithm and search computing. Our proposed method keeps the high estimation accuracy more closely to "FS" quality in comparison with "DS" and "HEXBS" fast motion estimation algorithm.
