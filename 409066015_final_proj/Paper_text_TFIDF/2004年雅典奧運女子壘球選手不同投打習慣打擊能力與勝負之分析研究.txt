2004年雅典奧運女子壘球選手不同投打習慣打擊能力與勝負之分析研究
雅典奧運、壘球
摘 要本研究是以2004年雅典奧林匹克運動會女子壘球比賽的美國、日本、中華台北、中國、加拿大、澳洲、希臘、義大利共八支參賽球隊實際上場參與打擊共95名選手之比賽記錄為範圍。利用皮爾遜積差相關、獨立樣本單因子變異數分析和薛費法（Scheffe）事後比較與t考驗等統計方法，經分析結果得到下列結論：一、  壘球運動的打擊率、上壘率、長打率與勝率皆呈顯著相關（P＜.05）。二、  各隊選手在打擊率、上壘率、長打率達顯著差異(P＜.05)。三、  不同投打習慣選手在打擊率、上壘率、長打率與勝率之關係（一）右投右打之選手在打擊率、上壘率與勝率皆未呈顯著相關（P＞.05）；長打率呈顯著相關(P＜.05)。（二）右投左打之選手在打擊率、上壘率、長打率與勝率皆呈顯著相關(P＜.05)。（三）左投左打之選手在打擊率、上壘率、長打率與勝率皆未達顯著相關（P＞.05）。四、  不同投打習慣選手在打擊率達顯著差異(P＜.05)；上壘率與長打率皆未達顯著差異（P＞.05)。五、  不同打擊區選手在打擊率、上壘率、長打率皆未達顯著差異(P＞.05)。
AbstractThe research is done according to the competition records of the ninety-five players in total from eight softball teams, including U.S.A, Japan,Chinese Taipei,China,Canada,Australia,Greece,Italy those actually participated in women’s softball contest in 2004 Olympic Games. This study utilizes the t-test, One – Way ANOVA, and Scheffer method and the K. Person Product-Moment correlation to analyze the available data and reaches the following conclusion1.  The percentage of game won positively correlates with batting average,on base percentage,slugging average (P<.05).2.  Players performance in batting average,on base percentage and slugging average are significantly different (P<.05).3.  The correlation among batting average, on base percentage, slugging average, and winning percentage in different players with different batting habits.（1）  For the right-handed-hitting-against-right-handed-pitching batter, the batting average, on base percentage, and winning percentage do not reveal strong correlations (P> .05), but the slugging average has a strong correlation with the winning percentage (P<.05).（2）  For the left-handed-hitting-against-right-handed-pitching batter, the batting average, on base percentage, and the slugging average all reveal strong correlations with the winning percentage (P<.05).（3）  For the left-handed-hitting-against-left-handed-pitching batter, the batting average, on base percentage, and the slugging average all do not have strong correlations with the winning percentage (P>.05).4.  Players with different throwing-batting habits show no different in on base percentage, and slugging average (P>.05). Batting average analysis of variance reaches (P<.05).5.  Players in different batting areas have similar performances in batting average,on base percentage, and slugging average (P>.05).
