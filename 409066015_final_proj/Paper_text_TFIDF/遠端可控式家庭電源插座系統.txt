遠端可控式家庭電源插座系統
Remotely Controllable Outlet System for Home Power Management
藍芽、mcu、伺服器
在本篇論文中，我們設計家庭電源管理遠端可控式插座系統。這個系統包含了4個區塊：藍芽電源控制插座模組(BPCOM, Bluetooth Power-Controlled Outlet Module)，家庭伺服器及網路遠端控制及GSM手機控制。藍芽是一種無線系統，可以讓控制家庭電源不須設置新的線路而達到無線化。BPCOM整合多個AC電源插座，和一個簡易低耗電的微控制器(MCU)來實現電源開關(On Off)插座。除此之外，使用霍爾電流元件做為電源偵測電路以傳回家庭電源的狀態至控制器。我們使用Visual Basic 6.0做為家庭伺服器的軟體，可提供圖形使用者界面(GUI)，讓使用者容易監控家庭電器電源開關。另外，結合藍芽做為家庭伺服器及微控制器之間接收與傳輸資料的通道。在遠端控制方面，我們也提供使用者透過網路及GSM簡訊(SMS, Short Message Service)連結家庭伺服器，以達到隨時隨地都可控制家庭電源開關的能力。
In this paper, we describe a remotely controllable outlet system which includes a mechanism for home power management. The system consists of four blocks the Bluetooth Power-Controlled Outlet Module (BPCOM), the home server, the remote control and the GSM channel. The Bluetooth wireless system can control the home electric appliances wirelessly at home without the addition of any new wiring. The BPCOM integrates the multiple AC power sockets and a simple plug-in low-power microcontroller performs the power on off switching of the sockets. In addition, the power detection circuits are used by the Hall current transducer to verify the home electric appliances power on off status. For the power management module of the home server, we designed the software module by Visual Basic 6.0, which supports the Graphic User Interface (GUI) to let users easily monitor whether the home electric appliances power is on or off. We also integrate Bluetooth to receive transmit between the home server and the microcontroller. The remote control also provides the users with the ability to control home appliances power on off through the Internet and the GSM channel linked to the home server in order to receive the SMS (Short Message Service) for the power management of the home electric appliances power on off anytime and anywhere.
