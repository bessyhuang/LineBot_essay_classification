以影像為基礎之距離與面積量測系統
Image-Based Distance and Area Measuring System
距離量測法、面積量測法、電壓比較器

在本論文中，提出一種簡單創新的量測系統，經由CCD攝影機，使用掃描線計時的方法，可測得CCD攝影機到物體的距離和該物體的面積。此方法並不需要儲存全畫面的影像資料，也不必使用到複雜的圖形辨識演算法，只需要一些簡單的電路和公式就可以快速完成距離和面積的量測。在距離量測法上，當CCD攝影機在不同距離時，將產生不同的影像訊號，再利用計時電路來計算一條水平掃描線佔物體投影面積的時間比例，並透過簡單的計算，即可得到量測的距離。在面積量測法上，只要利用一個計時電路來計算水平掃描線佔物體投影面積的時間，將所有的時間累加起來，並經過簡單的代數關係式，即可得到量測的面積。透過實驗結果可以得知系統的可靠性很高，在結果上有可以接受的準確度。本論文的主要特色乃在於，只使用到一條水平掃描線上的影像資料，便能得知攝影系統到待測物之間的距離。
In this thesis, a novel measuring system using a scan-counter method via a CCD camera is proposed. The proposed measuring system is an easy-to-construct system to measure the distance between a CCD camera and an object, and to measure the projected area of the object. This measuring method needs not store huge amounts of image data nor use any pattern recognition approach. It quickly obtains the distance and the projected area using simple circuits and formulas. For distance measurement, The CCD in the different distance, It have the different image signal, circuits count the number of external clock pulses of the horizontal scan line covering the projected area of the object. Therefore, the actual distance from the CCD camera to the object can be calculated from a simple formula. For area measurement, circuits count the number of external clock pulses of the horizontal scan lines covering the projected area of the object. Then, the projected area of the object is calculated from simple algebraic formulas. Finally, several experimental results demonstrate the effectiveness of the proposed system.
