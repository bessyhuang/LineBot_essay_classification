慢跑與爬坡之地面反作用力分析研究
A Study on Ground Reaction Force with Regard to Jogging and Uphill Walking
地面反作用力、足底壓力、步態
運動休閒參與行為的研究指出，人們較常參與的活動為慢跑與爬山健行，但並未顯示何項運動，可以在較低的運動傷害發生率下，達到運動的效果。本研究利用Gaitway測力跑步機，擷取受試者在平地慢跑與上坡走路的步態數據，以探討其地面反作用力相關參數之變化。受試者為64名大學生（平均年齡 22.61.35 歲、體重 67.3 14.55公斤、身高 170.48.62 公分）。實驗過程中，每位受試者在消耗等量卡路里的情況下，進行平地慢跑（速度8 Km h）與上坡走路（速度4 Km h、坡度16%）兩階段步態資料收集，並利用Gaitway分析軟體獲取地面反作用力相關數據。實驗結果顯示：一、平地慢跑的雙腳平均最大地面反用力為2.29倍體重，而上坡走路的雙腳平均最大地面反作用力分別為1.14倍體重，顯示平地慢跑時足部所需承受的力量大於上坡走路，且達顯著差異（p<0.05）。二、上坡走路時的衝量大於平地慢跑時的衝量（0.540.37），達顯著差異(p<0.05)。三、平地慢跑的第一峰值大於上坡走路的第一峰值（2.291.10），達顯著差異 (p<0.05)。四、平地慢跑達第一峰值時間平均為0.12秒，比上坡走路的0.17秒快，且達顯著差異 (p<0.05)。五、平地慢跑負荷率大於上坡走路的負荷率（24.698.49），達顯著差異 (p<0.05)，平地慢跑為上坡走路之2.9倍。六、平地慢跑之退化率大於上坡走路時的退化率（15.398.36），達顯著差異 (p<0.05)，平地慢跑為上坡走路的1.84倍。七、各項變數的雙腳相稱值呈現不穩定的狀況，標準差極大的情況下，若加以比較，則無實質意義。    根據上述發現，筆者建議若是平常人在選擇運動項目時，若以慢跑或爬山健行為選項時，可選擇爬山健行，對足部的負荷明顯小於平地慢跑運動，可降低運動傷害的風險，並達到近似的運動效果。
Studies of sport and recreation participation behavior show that activities that people often participate in are jogging and hiking. However previous studies do not indicate sport activity involves a lower injury risk yet produces optimal athletic benefit. In this study, sixty four young healthy subjects (22.61.35 yrs., 67.314.55 kg.) were tested on a Gaitway treadmill to collect data about the ground reaction force (GRF). The ground reaction force in both jogging (8km hr, 0%) and uphill walking (4km hr, 16%) were tested. The results showed that1.The GRF of jogging equals about two times that of uphill walking.2.The impulse in uphill walking is greater than the impulse in jog (0.540.37).3.The first peak force in jogging is higher than that of uphill walking (2.291.10).4.The time to first peak of jogging is 0.12s, and it is faster than uphill walking (0.17s).5.The weight acceptance rate of jogging is higher than that of uphill walking (24.698.49).6.The push off rate of jogging is greater than that of uphill walking (15.398.36).7.The symmetry values in the study were unstable. The standard deviations were higher than the average values, thus we the comparison of the symmetry of jogging with uphill walking was not adoptable.According to the results, excluding the finding of the symmetry values, all findings pointed to significant differences between jogging and uphill walking. When people are given the option of jogging or uphill walking, they ought to choose uphill walking as the best leisure sport. Uphill walking causes a lower risk of injury as well as producing optimal physical benefit because the vertical GRF is significantly lower.
