企業運用智慧型代理人影響電子商務顧客信任之研究
The Study of Influencing Customer Trust in E-commerce by Intelligent Agent
電子商務、智慧型代理人、信任
電子商務沒有真正成功的主要原因在於缺乏顧客信任；由於電子商務具有許多潛在的風險，使得顧客不易對於虛擬環境的無實體商店產生良好的信任感，無法建立顧客信任，因而也影響了電子商務的發展。將智慧型代理人運用於電子商務，使其可以幫助顧客，增進產品選擇、廠商選擇及協商的效率，或許可以成為這些問題的解決方案。本研究的主要目的即是探討企業運用智慧型代理人是否會影響顧客對電子商務網站的信任程度。    透過實驗法中的情境法，將受測者分配到研究實驗網站─手機購物網站的八組實驗情境當中，來研究、驗證企業運用智慧型代理人影響電子商務信任之研究架構。經由實驗分析結果得知，企業於電子商務購買決策流程中的產品選擇階段、協商階段運用智慧型代理人，以提升顧客在選擇產品上及協商時的效率，因而增加顧客對電子商務的信任，此研究結果提供企業電子商務網站一個發展的方向，它將可為企業帶來更多的競爭優勢及商業利機，同時也有助於未來電子商務的發展。
Customer trust is emerging as a key element of success in B2C e-commerce environment. However, e-commerce have a lot of potential risks, making customers to be difficult to have trust in virtual environment, therefore influence the development of e-commerce. A number of researches have demonstrated that degrees and impact of customer trust on purchase intentions change with online experience. Intelligent agents help automate a variety of tasks included in buying and selling products over Internet. In this study, we focus on Intelligent agents assisting online customers in product brokering stage, merchant brokering stage and negotiation stage, guiding consumer buying behavior to improve their online experience.    A scenario experiment was conducted to investigate the impact on online customer trust applying intelligent agent. We found that online vendors should apply intelligent agent technology in product brokering stage and negotiation stage to tone up customer trust in e-commerce all the time. This study will provide e-commerce wesite with the direction of development, bringing more competitive advantage and commercial benefit; moreover, it will assist the development of e-commerce in the future.
