普瑞德－威利氏症候群（Prader-Willisyndrome）病患家屬照護經驗之研究
The Caring Experiences of Families of Children with Prader-Willi Syndrome
小胖威利氏症、照護經驗
本研究之目的為深入探討普瑞德－威利氏症候群（Prader-Willi syndrome；PWS）病患家屬之照護經驗。採立意取樣，選取診斷確立為普瑞德－威利氏症候群病患的七位成年家屬作為研究對象，運用現象學研究法，以深入訪談的方式收集資料，並將訪談錄音內容謄寫成逐字稿，採Giorgi的現象學分析方法分析資料，透過解釋及分析家屬的述說，將家屬之照護經驗與感受展現。研究結果顯示家屬之照護經驗可分為四個主題，一.疾病進展難料：包含「詭變的生理特徵」、「失序的行為表現」及「殘缺的發展情境」；二.就醫歷程艱苦：包含「慌亂波折的診斷過程」、「緩不濟急的醫療資源」及「難以認同的醫療服務」；三.生活處境坎坷：包含「孩子遭受排擠」及「社會支持貧瘠」；四.自身天職難為：包含「孤獨的承擔」、「無盡的擔憂」及「維持自我衡定」。研究結果真實展現家屬之照顧經驗，使醫護人員瞭解家屬之照護困境與需求並協助之，將可提昇護理人員及家屬的照護品質，亦可作為醫療團隊落實「以家庭為中心」及「本土化」的家庭照顧之參考。
This research is to explore the experiences of families in the care of their children with Prader-Willi Syndrome. The experiences from one father and six mothers were collected and analyzed by Giorgi phenomenological method. Individual interview was arranged and audiotaped under natural circumstance. The results were integrated to into four phenomenological themes  (1) unknowable of disease progress including「changeful of physiological characteristics」 , 「out-of-sequence of behaviors」, and「incomplete of development」; (2) arduous of seeking for medical treatment process  including 「Diagnose process is flurried」, 「medical resources is not enough to save a critical situation」, and 「unreliable of medical services」; (3) life is full of frustration including 「children being excluded」, and 「lack of social supports」; (4) 「bounden duty is embarrassing」 including 「a solitary sufferer」、「endless worry」, and 「maintaining a stable ego」. This research to show the caring experiences of families, helps health care professionals getting an in-depth understanding of the families’ predicaments and needs, then provide suitable help, and even promoting a family-centered, cultural sensitive “Family Nurse” system for Taiwan.
