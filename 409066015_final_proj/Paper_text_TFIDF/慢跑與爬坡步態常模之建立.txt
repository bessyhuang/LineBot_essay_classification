慢跑與爬坡步態常模之建立
Establishing Normative Data of Gait Parameters in Jogging and Uphill Walking
步態、第一峰值、步態分析
本研究主要目的在測量國內大學青年人，分析其在不同坡度下跑步與走路的時間及距離、地面反作用力與心跳率等相關步態資訊，以期瞭解跑步與上坡之相關步態並建立一套常模。共收集64位大學在學青年（男生44位，女生20位），身高170.44  8.62公分、體重67.25  14.55公斤、年齡22.61  1.35歲。實驗中使用Gaitway測力跑步機分析連續性步態來進行評估。實驗時要求受試者以平地8公里跑步、4公里約16%上坡走步，擷取兩種不同速度與坡度的相關步態資料進行敘述性統計分析。結果顯示，本研究測量的相關步態資訊包括心跳率、最大力量、接觸地面時間、第一峰值、達第一峰值所需時間、第二峰值、跨步長、單步長、退化率、負荷率、相稱值，個體間變異係數多小於50%，顯示在受試者之間一致性很高，應該可以做為青年人慢跑與上坡走路之步態常模。
The purpose of this study is to establish the normative data of the temporal- distance data ,the ground reaction forces and heart rate in different speed jogging and uphill walking.Using Gaitway treadmill analysis system, to collect each of 64 young university students ( 22.61  1.35years, height 170.44  8.62 cm, weight 67.25  14.55kg ) experiment requests to jogging in speed 8 km h , uphill walking in speed 4 km h , picks up two kind of speeds gait data to proceed narration statistical analysis.With appropriate normalization of the measured gait parameters, the results of this study showed the inter-subject coefficients of variation of most parameters in all subjects were below 50%.This result validates the applicability of the normative data of the gait parameters during first pick force, second pick force, stride length, stride time, step length, step time , contact time , time to first pick, push off rate, weight acceptance rate, symmetry. During temporal-distance data, ground reaction forces and heart rate validates the applicability of the normative data of the gait parameters in young students.
