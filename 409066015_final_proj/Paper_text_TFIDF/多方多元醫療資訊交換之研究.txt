多方多元醫療資訊交換之研究
The Study for the Exchange of Multi-Directional Multiple Healthcare Information
健康資訊交換第七層協定、臨床文件架構、醫療數位元影像通訊標準、網路服務、軟體代理人
隨著社會進步以及民眾生活素質的提昇，醫療品質仍是代表國家發展上的重要指標，不論是從政府機構、醫療院所或者研究單位為出發點，都努力的使全國人民能得到更好的醫療服務品質、醫療模式，期許民眾在需要醫療資源時能得到最好的照顧。如此的醫療院所都不斷的強化本身的服務，以及引用新的醫療科技與系統來朝此一目標邁進，然而也因此造成醫療產業內資訊系統之紛亂，許多醫療資訊不能在組織間、單位間甚至系統間達到互通，造成許多醫療資源無謂的浪費，以及醫療資訊無法及時有效的傳達，所幸在法規及科技的努力下，此一問題已經逐漸獲得改善了，HL7、DICOM的發展及推廣，讓醫療資訊及醫療媒體影像上有了一致的標準，而能進一步達成醫療資訊在系統間及組織間的流通。本研究即以醫療組織間資訊流通為方向，提供一整合性的資料傳遞方式來試圖解決醫療院所間資料交換議題，藉由HL7 RIM、CDA、DICOM為資料基礎，配合代理人的協同運作以及網路服務（Web Services）兩者來加以輔助，使醫療資訊／影像能無礙的在醫療體系內傳遞，研究中以轉診以及疫情通報為實作方向，進行雛型系統的建立與實驗，期望藉由此來降低醫療組織間資訊交換的複雜度，並提昇資訊內容的完整度，讓正在不斷向上攀升的醫療服務品質能得以延續。
The objectives of this research aim to enhancement the system interoperability and information compatibility among healthcare institutions involved in electronic patient referral.This paper presents an experimental system implementation to support patient information interchanging in patient referral and disease report between healthcare institutions. The system is implemented based on an agent-based architecture by adopting Health Level 7, Clinic Data Architecture,and Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine v3.0 as well as leveraging the use of advanced information technologies. We find HL7 V3.0 provides a thorough component-based reference information model with better transportability, scalability, integration, interpretability but it is also more difficult and complicate to implement but overall it is the trend and unique HL7 standard we need to adopt.The major information technologies used are Web services, software agents, and objected-oriented methodologies whereas the main standards of medical informatics adopted are Health Level 7, Clinic Data Architecture, and Digital Imaging and Communications in Medicine.The result shows the approach used can enhance higher system interoperability, scalability, integration as well as the efficiency and compatibility of information delivery.
