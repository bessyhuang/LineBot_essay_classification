李克特量表分數轉換之研究—以憂鬱情緒量表為例
A Study on Likert Scale Data Transformation— Using Melancholia Scale as an Example
李克特量表、憂鬱情緒、類別資料

本研究以量表資料之分數轉換處理為主要研究課題，並以國中體育班及普通班學生之憂鬱情緒量表資料為例，探討整數值計分及E.J.Snell所建議之計分方式對分析結果之影響。本研究之主要研究結果如下：一、李克特量表原始計分與類別資料轉換得分之信度都相同，均屬於高信度。二、兩種不同的計分方式之相關性分析，多為正相關。三、兩種不同的計分方式之因素分析，傳統計分法可解釋全體變數的56.52%，類別資料轉換計分的累積變異解釋量則為56.438%。四、數值的轉換對資料分析結果所造成的差異不大。五、在有競爭性的壓力之下，運動亦會是產生憂鬱的來源。針對以上結果，提供下列建議以做為未來研究的參考：一、以經過醫師判斷，實際患有憂鬱症患者為研究對象，以便驗證轉換後之資料的實際判別度。二、未來可進一步用FUZZY理論來做資料轉換以利進行比較分析。
The purpose of this study is to investigate the effects upon the transformation of Likert Scale, and to discuss the transformation methods regarding to the difference between the students in the athletic class and the ordinary students in different schools. Take the case study of Melancholia Scale and compare Likert Scale scores with category transformation scores suggested by E.J.Snell. The major findings of the research are summarized as the following：1. The coefficient of reliability of Likert Scale scores and the category transformation scores is the same. They both have high reliability。2. The analysis of their correlation is positive。3. The conclusion of the factor analysis is： The Likert Scale scoring method can explain 56.52% of variables, and the category transformation scoring method can explain 56.438% of cumulative variance.4. There is not much difference between the results of using either method to analyze.5. Under the pressure of competition, exercise is also the source of Melancholia.Based on the research findings, the recommendations for further research are：1. To find people who suffer Melancholia to be your study targets is helpful.2. The further direction of research may use FUZZY to make the transformation.
