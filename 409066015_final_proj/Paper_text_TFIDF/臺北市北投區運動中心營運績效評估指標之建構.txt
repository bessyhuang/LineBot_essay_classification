臺北市北投區運動中心營運績效評估指標之建構
Development Performance Appraisal of Taipei City BEITOU Sports Center
運動中心、績效評估、平衡計分卡
臺北市北投區運動中心營運績效評估指標之建構輔仁大學體育學系碩士班研究生：徐維聯                         指導教授：楊志顯 博士                            摘要本研究以質性研究方式，透過平衡計分卡來規劃臺北市北投區運動中心的績效評估指標，使用半結構式訪談、文獻及資料整理、經營策略規劃來進行資料收集，以資料三角測定加以詮釋和討論所蒐集資料，其研究結論如下：財務構面策略主題為建立穩固的財務基礎，包括1個目標、2個落後指標、1個領先指標。顧客構面策略主題為滿意的顧客消費經驗、建立良好民間關係，包括5個目標、8個落後指標、4個領先指標。內部流程策略主題為完善的服務作業流程、良好的顧客管理、發展有效的創新流程、健康與安全的社會責任，包括8個目標、6個落後指標、9個領先指標。學習與成長策略主題為優異的工作團隊、持續改善與學習的組織，包括4個目標、3個落後指標、4個領先指標。關鍵詞：運動中心、績效評估、平衡計分卡論文總頁數：158
Development Performance Appraisal of Taipei CityBEITOU Sports CenterGraduate Institute of Physical Education, Fu Jen Catholic UniversityStudent：Hsu-Wei Lien        Advisor：Dr. Chih-Hsien YangABSTRACTThis study was a qualitative research that utilized the application of balanced scorecard to develop the indicators of operative performance appraisal for Taipei City BEITOU Sports Center. The study was conducted through semi-structured interviews, analysis for the literatures, and strategic planning to gather materials. Next, data triangulation was used to analyze and explain the data. Here comes out the conclusionsThe strategic theme of financial perspective was to build a stable financial groundwork, which included one target, two lag indicators, and one lead indicator. The strategic themes of customer perspective were satisfyingly consuming experience and popular relationship, including five targets, eight lag indicators and four lead indicators. The strategic themes of internal business-process perspective are thoroughly service procedures, completely customer management, developing effectively creative procedure, healthily and safely social responsibility, including eight targets, six lag indicators and nine lead indicators. Learning and growth perspective strategic themes are superior working group, improving and learning organization, including four targets, three lag indicators and four lead indicators.Key words Sports Center, Performance Appraisal, Balanced ScorecardTotal pages 158
