本國銀行經營績效研究
On the Operational Performance of the Domestic Banks in Taiwan.
銀行、經營績效、轉換函數、財務指標

銀行經營績效之評估，一方面提供銀行業良性的競爭；另一方面，可強化銀行的監理審查，降低銀行風險的擴大危機；亦可提供投資大眾作參考，故本研究目的是想要評估國內上市之中型銀行的經營績效，以分數的方式呈現各銀行的經營績效。本研究經由財務指標的意義，可得知財務指標有比率與百分比二種，本研究欲將比率型財務指標，依其相對性或絶對性來決定是否予以標準化，再透過適當函數轉換成百分制，最後分別給予五大構面之權重求算各家銀行整體分數。本研究資料為民國93年之年度財務資料，研究對象為大眾商業銀行、中國農民銀行、聯邦商業銀行、遠東商業銀行、萬泰商業銀行、安泰商業銀行、新竹國際商業銀行、台中商業銀行、中華商業銀行等9家銀行。本研究結果，各家銀行整體的經營績效前三名依序為：安泰商銀、萬泰商銀、農民銀行。
The evaluation of banking operational performance, one side, it can afford positive competition among banking, on the other hand, it can intensify intendance and examination of banking as well; further more, it can be as the reference to investors. So the purpose of this research is to evaluate the operational performance of Taiwan middle size banks in the stock market, use the “grade” in order to present the operational performance in each bank.This research through the meaning of finance index sign, there are two financial indexes, ratio and percentage.This research want to using the ratio type finance index sign depends on its relativity or absolute to decide whether standardize, then through appropriate function conversion become hundred-mark system, finally, give the weight of five dimensions to calculate each score with whole bank. The research period is the whole year of 2004, and the sampling banks include 9 banks are Ta Chong Bank, Farmer Bank, Union Bank Of Taiwan, Far Eastern International Bank, Cosmos Bank, En Tie Commercial Bank, Hsin Chu International Bank, Tai Chung Commercial Bank, The Chinese Bank.The empirical research shows the operational performance of our all sample banks in Taiwan, the first 3 banks are En Tie Commercial Bank, Cosmos Bank, Farmer Bank.
