訂製品成本估算及訂價技術之研究
The study of Cost-Estimating and Pricing of Customizing Products
訂製品、成本估算、訂價估算
在微利時代的環境下，低成本及差異化已是企業競爭優勢所在，藉資訊技術將顧客和企業間、企業和供應商間之資訊快速傳遞及整合，然而現行報價系統，採標準訂價方式計價，無法隨著實際生產情況估算，缺乏彈性，無法確定訂價是否能創造利潤，所以建立客製化產品之成本估算模式。    本研究主要探討：在大量客製化下生產之訂製品，將生產成本及訂價相關因素建立估算模式，可分為純粹標準化、部份客製化、完全客製化三種模式，模擬直接材料及生產製程客製化程度，彙整有關成本估算的模式，並提出影響各生產成本的因素，可提供訂製品價格訂定資訊。    經過個案模擬後，發現此估算模式的效益為：1.建構出訂製品的價格估算模式，供企業界參考。2.以實例公司的資料，進行訂製品的成本估算及價格估算的實證，獲得訂製品的成本、訂價及利潤相關的實證資料可供實務界參考。
Low-Cost and Differentiation have been enterprise's competitive advantage in this “mini-profit margin era”. With technological improvements in response to customers’ demand and combine the information among the supplier. We found the fixed pricing of current business application systems unable to estimate with the actual condition of production.     This research wants to build a cost-estimating and pricing model of customizing products. This model divides material into three types Pure Standardization, Partly Customization, and Pure Customization. We change some factors of production costs and customize processes, that can get some importantly information about costing and pricing. We have two results such as1.Build the mold which Cost-Estimating and Pricing of customizing products for business reference.2.Use the case company’s data to examine the mold, it will provide a reference to get the real example for costs, prices and profits of customizing products.
