以學生-問題分析法診斷學生認知狀態之情境感知學習-以計算機概論教學為例
Context Aware Learning by Diagnosing Student's Cognitive States with the Student-Problem Analysis -A Case Study on Introductory Computer Science Course
情境感知、成敗歸因、Student-Problem Table
在數位學習環境下，教學成效的提升比科技效果的呈現還重要，教學效果的良窳決定於師生間的互動。因此教師必須根據個人經驗進行晤談及觀察等取得學生的認知狀態，以改善教學。為了取得學生認知狀態，本研究之學生「學習狀態」採用Weiner的成敗歸因穩定向度，應用成就測驗的原始資料，藉由Student-Problem Table的學生注意係數及答對率做為自變項。由成敗歸因量表取得學生實際學習狀態，以積差相關証明Student-Problem Table學生注意係數與成敗歸因穩定向度兩者具顯著相關。為預測學習狀態，本論文採單變數二分判定法及多變數區別分析法等兩種方法，經過在北市高職的計算機概論課程之實驗比較，多變數區別分析法可達到最好的區別率。
In the e-learning environments, the promotion of teaching results is more important than the adoption of technical. Interaction between teachers and students can greatly influence the teaching results. The teacher has to improve the teaching according to individual experience, meeting, observation, and so on to obtain students’ cognitive states. This thesis adopts Weiner’s attribution theory and defines some states cognitive states. Then, Student-Problem Table is used to analyze students' coefficients of attention and ratio of correct answers. We use scale of attributions to obtain the students’ cognitive states Pearson's product-moment correlation is utilized to show the correspondence between caution index of students and attribution theory stability. In order to forecast the cognitive state, univariable bisection method and multivariable discriminate analysis are proposed. We conduct experiments on a course of introductory computer science in a senior high school in Taipei. Experimental results show that the multivariable discriminate analysis can achieve better discrimination rate.
