棒球投手、加速期、運動學分析
棒球投手投球動作之運動學分析輔仁大學體育學系碩士班研究生：林郁捷                                  指導教授：陳太正 教授中文摘要本研究之目的在於比較不同層級投手，在全力投擲直球時，身體動作加速期之運動學參數的差異。本研究使用兩台JVC9800數位攝影機進行受試者投球動作影像之收集影像資料，以攝影法分析十二名分屬不同層級之優秀投手，包括職棒投手二名、大學投手五名、高中投手五名。並將實驗所得的影像資料，透過Silicon Coach力學分析系統進行各參數的運算，以Windows XP套裝軟體中的Excel先求取平均值與標準差再利用SPSS 10.0統計套裝軟體，並以皮爾森積差相關、單因子變異數分析(one-way ANOVA)、薛費法(Scheffe)事後比較進行統計分析，所得結果如下：一、高中組、大學組、職棒組等三級投手投球動作加速期時間，均呈顯著相關(P﹤.01)。組間效果的考驗並未達到顯著性(P>.05)。二、高中組、大學組、職棒組等三級投手投球球速，均呈顯著相關(P﹤.01)。組間效果的考驗並未達到顯著性(P>.05)。三、高中組、大學組、職棒組等三級投手投球動作重心總位移，均呈顯著相關(P﹤.01)。組間效果的考驗達到顯著水準並有顯著差異存在(P<.05)。由此觀之，處於不同階段的投手，身高比例的不同，對於投球動作重心的總位移有差異。四、高中組、大學組、職棒組等三級投手投球動作加速期投球臂肩、肘、腕關節及指尖，在球離手瞬間的速度，均呈顯著相關(P﹤.01)。組間效果的考驗並未達到顯著性(P>.05)。五、高中組、大學組、職棒組等三級投手投球動作加速期投球臂肘關節在加速期中的角度，均呈顯著相關(P﹤.01)。組間效果的考驗並未達到顯著性(P>.05)。關鍵詞：棒球投手、加速期、運動學分析論文總頁數：65
The Kinematics analysis of baseball pitching motionGraduate Institute of Physical Education, Fu Jen Catholic UniversityResearcher：Lin Yu-Jie                         Advisor：Chen Tai-ChengAbstractThe purpose of this study was to compare the differences of body movement  parameters in acceleration phase of three different groups of pitchers, when they pitched ball at their most effort. This study applied two digital cameras to collect the visual data of 2 professional pitchers, 5 university pitchers and 5 senior high school pitchers. And calculate the visual data into parameters by Silicon Coach mechanic analysis system. The parameters was analyzed by descriptive statistics, person product-moment correlation, one –way ANOVA and Scheffe multiple comparison by package statistics software SPSS 10.0. The findings of this study concluded as follow.〈一〉there is positive correlation in three groups in 「time 」parameter (P﹤.01), and no significant difference between groups, (P>.05).〈二〉there is positive correlation in three groups in 「speed」parameter (P﹤.01), and no significant difference between groups, (P>.05).〈三〉there is positive correlation in three groups in 「gravity shift 」parameter (P﹤.01), and there is significant difference between groups, (P<05). It means pitchers in different level make different gravity shift, because of the different height.〈四〉there is positive correlation in acceleration phase on 「arm shoulder」、「elbow」、「wrist」、「finger tips」and  「the speed in the moment of release」 (P﹤.01) in three groups, and no significant difference between groups, (P>.05).〈五〉there is positive correlation in acceleration phase on「angle of elbow」in three groups, (P﹤.01), and no significant difference between groups, (P>.05).Key words baseball pitcher, acceleration phase, Kinematics analysis Total pages：65
