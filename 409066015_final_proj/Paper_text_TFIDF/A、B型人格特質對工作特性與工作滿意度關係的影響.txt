A、B型人格特質對工作特性與工作滿意度關係的影響
The Effects of Type A B personality on the Relationship between Job Characteristic and Job Satisfaction.
人格特質、工作特性、工作滿意度
企業留才已成為企業提升競爭力的重要議題，而其中提升工作滿意度是一項留才的重要方法。企業應將適合的工作分配予合適的人格者以提升其工作滿意度。本研究即將以激勵濳能分數為自變數，A、B型人格特質為干擾變數，探討其對工作滿意度的影響，首先整理國內外相關文獻，作為參考之依據。再對有工作經驗者進行調查，共回收311份問卷，經由統計分析軟體SPSS13.0進行分析，經過信度分析(Reliability Analysis)、集群分析(Cluster Analysis)以及二因子變異數分析(Two Way ANOVA)檢定後，本研究發現：一、  人格特質對工作特性與工作滿意度的關係具有干擾效果二、  A型人格在工作自主性較低之工作有較B型人格有工作滿意度；B型人格在工作自主性較高之工作有較高工作滿意度三、  A型人格在工作回饋性較低之工作有較B型人格有工作滿意度；B型人格在工作回饋性較高之工作有較高工作滿意度
Employees preservation has become the most significant topic for cooperation to increase its competitive advantage.  It is a relatively important element to preserve the employees by improving job satisfaction.  The employer should have provided the employees with the most suitable Jobs to improve their job satisfaction.  In this research, motivating potential scores will be the independent variable and the type A B personality will be the moderate variable in order to confer the influence of job satisfaction.  First of all, organize the domestic and foreign related references as basic resources.  In addition, market research toward the persons under current employment, and collect back total of 311 questionnaires.  At the end, analyze the data through SPSS 13.0 software, continually through reliability analysis; cluster analysis and two way ANOVA come out with final findings shown as below1.  Personality does affect the job characteristic and job satisfaction.2.  Type A personality tends to have lower job autonomy, hence lower job satisfaction than Type B personality； Type B personality tends to have higher job autonomy, hence higher job satisfaction.3.  Type A personality tends to have lower job compensation, hence lower job satisfaction than type B personality； type B personality tends to have higher job compensation, hence higher job satisfaction.
